🎨 Color Harmony

Color harmony is the art of combining colors in a way that feels balanced, pleasing, and visually appealing. It helps outfits (or designs) look stylish and well-coordinated.

🌑 Monochromatic

Using different shades, tones, and tints of a single color. This approach creates a cohesive and elegant look.

🎯 Complementary

Colors that sit opposite each other on the color wheel. This scheme offers high contrast and can make an outfit stand out.

🔀 Split-Complementary

One main color + the two colors next to its opposite. This scheme offers strong visual contrast without being as intense as a complementary color scheme.

🌿 Analogous

Colors that sit next to each other on the color wheel. Smooth, Natural, Harmonious

🔺 Triadic

Three colors evenly spaced on the color wheel. This scheme is vibrant and offers a high level of contrast while maintaining harmony.

🔲 Tetradic

Two pairs of complementary colors. Rich, colorful, and flexible, but needs balance.
